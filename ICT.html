ICT(Introductory computing technology)

Information and communications technology (ICT) is an extensional term for information technology (IT)
that stresses the role of unified communications and the integration of telecommunications
(telephone lines and wireless signals) and computers, as well as necessary enterprise software,
middleware, storage, and audio-visual systems, which enable users to access, store, transmit, and manipulate
information.
